- Favor explanations that teach the mechanism, not just the conclusion

## Core Principles

- Data quality often matters as much as model cleverness

- Good tools accelerate thought, not just execution

## Watchouts

- Keep practical constraints visible, not just elegant decomposition
- Do not assume the most educational path is always the fastest path
